Tattoo Artist

I'm the kind of person who isn't afraid of challenges.

Ritesh Sawant

I got into tattooing through growing up on bike rides listening to old songs in a small town name is Bhandara. My style is a made from a few aspects that inspire me, street art, traditional tattooing, pop culture and nostalgia for sure. In the beginning of my career I was more leaning towards the new school side of things but always loved bright colours and trippy drippy work. As the years gone on I have shifted to a more traditional heavy black approach with the bright colours and trippy aspects holding true throughout my career.
